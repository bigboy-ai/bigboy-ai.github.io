About this website and Video instruction

Hello! My name is Aiden and this is about my website. My websites content consist of having about 6 pages,
first the introduction, then the 5 cities. Each pages have at least one city with 6 popular places.

The purpose of my website and the benefits for using it is time management.
People such as families, tourist, or people visiting family members in Japan would make a list of how they are going to spend their holiday,
but Japan has so many places to visit, and because of that, some of them may miss out on the one's they would really enjoy.

However, this website saves your time by giving you a list of cities links that leads you to 6 areas that are very famous( Reviews from google),
there would also be collaspsible at the back on every popular places stated and once clicked, would show a brief description about it.
In addition, I also added a menu at the other 5 pages so that navigation is easy to move around.
