Kiyomizudera Temple🌸🏛️4.5 ★★★★⯪(62,083)

Kiyomizudera Temple is famous for its iconic,
nail-less wooden stage that extends from the main hall,
offering panoramic views of Kyoto's city and natural scenery

Fushimi Inari Shrine is most famous for its thousands of vibrant vermilion torii gates,
which create tunnels of red along the hiking trails of Mount Inari

Kinkakuji💧🏛️4.5 ★★★★⯪(61,511)

Kinkaku-ji, or the Golden Pavilion, is famous for its gold-leaf-covered exterior,
which reflects beautifully in the surrounding pond

Arashiyama🎍🌿4.5 ★★★★⯪(6,455)

Arashiyama is famous for its iconic Bamboo Grove,

that create a serene and otherworldly atmosphere

Gion Matsuri🎎🏯4.5 ★★★★⯪(188)
